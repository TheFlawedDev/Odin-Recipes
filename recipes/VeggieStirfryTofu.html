Quinoa and Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu


Indulge in a delicious and wholesome culinary journey with our Quinoa and Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu. This vibrant dish is a symphony of flavors and textures, perfectly suited for those striving to attain a lean and nourished body. Marinated cubes of firm tofu take on an irresistible savory essence, while the mix of crisp stir-fry vegetables—featuring an array of broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, and snap peas—adds a burst of color and nutrients. The concoction is artfully combined with cooked quinoa, offering a wholesome foundation that complements the dish's protein-rich profile. A medley of minced garlic and ginger infuses an aromatic zest, harmonizing with the sesame oil that envelops the ensemble. Topped with a drizzle of low-sodium soy sauce or tamari, this creation culminates in a balanced and savory sensation that's as good for your taste buds as it is for your physique. Garnished with chopped green onions and delicate sesame seeds, this Quinoa and Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu exemplifies the fusion of flavor and nutrition in every enticing bite.


Ingredients

  • Firm tofu, cubed
  • Cooked quinoa
  • Mixed stir-fry vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, snap peas, etc.)
  • Low-sodium soy sauce or tamari
  • Minced garlic and ginger
  • Olive oil or sesame oil for cooking
  • Optional: chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

How to make it:

  1. Press and cube the tofu, then marinate it in a mixture of low-sodium soy sauce, minced garlic, and minced ginger for at least 15 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil or sesame oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the marinated tofu cubes and cook until they are browned and slightly crispy. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed, and stir-fry the mixed vegetables until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Add the cooked quinoa to the pan and mix well with the vegetables.
  6. Return the tofu to the pan and stir everything together.
  7. Drizzle a bit of soy sauce or tamari over the stir-fry for flavor.
  8. Serve the quinoa and veggie stir-fry in bowls, and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
